Raspberry Pi Wifi Auto Reconnect

February 15, 2014
  1. Go to /etc/ifplugd/action.d/ and rename the ifupdown file to ifupdown.original
  2. Then do: cp /etc/wpa_supplicant/ifupdown.sh ./ifupdown
  3. Finally: sudo reboot
